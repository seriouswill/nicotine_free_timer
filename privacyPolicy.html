Privacy Policy for Nicotine Free Timer

Last updated: March 19, 2024

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Nicotine Free Timer ("we", "our", or "app") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our mobile application. (We do not collect any data from you).

Information We Collect

The Nicotine Free Timer app is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect or transmit any personal data to any external servers.

  • Local Storage Only: All information (including your name, nicotine type preference, usage records, and timer data) is stored locally on your device only.
  • No Data Collection: We do not collect analytics, usage statistics, or any personally identifiable information.

Permissions

Our app requires the following permissions:

  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS: Required to show timer notifications when the app is running in the background.
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE: Required to keep the timer running when the app is in the background.

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, data security is managed by your device's own security features. We recommend keeping your device secured with a PIN, pattern, or biometric authentication.

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Third-Party Services

Our app does not use any third-party services, analytics tools, or advertising networks that would collect data from users.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the Play Store listing for the Nicotine Free Timer app.
