About the App

Nicotine Free Timer is a simple yet powerful tool designed to help you quit nicotine products by tracking how long you've been nicotine-free and monitoring your daily usage. Whether you're trying to quit cigarettes, vaping, nicotine pouches, or gum, this app provides the motivation and tracking you need to succeed.
